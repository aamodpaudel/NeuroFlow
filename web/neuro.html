Designed for Everyone

ShopSimple follows Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1 AA) and neuro-inclusive design principles.

  • 🔤
    Single typeface Inter — readable, consistent, no decorative fonts competing for attention.
  • 🎨
    High contrast colours 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio throughout. Clear visual hierarchy.
  • 🚫
    No animations All CSS transitions and animations are disabled in this mode.
  • 📐
    Generous spacing Line height 1.9×, max-width 860px, large click targets (min 44×44px).
  • 🛑
    No popups or interruptions You decide when and how you interact. No timers, no pressure.
  • ⌨️
    Fully keyboard navigable Every element is reachable and usable with a keyboard. Focus indicators are always visible.
